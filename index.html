// Installation

Four steps to playtime.

From download to first launch in under 30 minutes.

1

Download the ISO

Click the download button above. The 5.6 GB ISO downloads directly to your machine.

2

Flash to USB

Use Ventoy, Rufus, or dd to write the ISO to a USB stick (8 GB or larger).

3

Boot from USB

Restart your computer, enter your BIOS boot menu (F12, F2, or Del), select the USB drive. Make sure UEFI mode is enabled.

4

Click "Install CobaltX"

Launch the installer from the desktop. Follow the wizard. Reboot. Game on.
